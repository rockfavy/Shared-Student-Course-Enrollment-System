The Student Course Enrollment System is a Blazor WebAssembly application designed to enable students to register, authenticate, browse available courses, enroll in courses, and manage their course enrollments. The system follows a clean architecture pattern with a Blazor WASM frontend communicating with a Minimal API backend, utilizing in-memory data storage for simplicity and rapid development.

At its foundation, the Student Course Enrollment System follows a feature-based architecture that organizes functionality into clear modules such as Authentication, Courses, and Enrollments. The system operates as a modern web application with a clear separation between the client-side Blazor WASM application and the server-side Minimal API, reducing complexity while maintaining scalability.

---

### The platform empowers students with:

#### 1. Secure Authentication
A registration and login system that allows students to securely access their course information and enrollment data.

#### 2. Course Browsing
A comprehensive view of all available courses with details such as course name, description, and capacity, enabling students to make informed enrollment decisions.

#### 3. Course Enrollment Management
The ability to enroll in multiple courses and view all enrolled courses in one place, with the flexibility to deregister when needed.

#### 4. Real-Time Enrollment Status
Immediate visibility into enrollment status, allowing students to track their course load and manage their academic schedule effectively.
