The Course Enrollment System is a web-based application designed to enable students to manage their course enrollments efficiently. The system provides a secure, user-friendly interface for students to browse available courses, enroll in courses, and manage their academic schedule.

---

### The platform empowers students with:

#### 1. Secure Access
A registration and authentication system that allows students to securely access their course information and enrollment data.

#### 2. Course Discovery
A comprehensive view of all available courses with details such as course name, description, and capacity, enabling students to make informed enrollment decisions.

#### 3. Enrollment Management
The ability to enroll in multiple courses and view all enrolled courses in one place, with the flexibility to deregister when needed.

#### 4. Real-Time Status
Immediate visibility into enrollment status, allowing students to track their course load and manage their academic schedule effectively.
