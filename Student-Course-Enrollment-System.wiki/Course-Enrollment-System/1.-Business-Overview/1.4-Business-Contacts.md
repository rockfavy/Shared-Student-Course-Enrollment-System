## Business Contacts

Key contacts and stakeholders for the Student Course Enrollment System.

## Project Stakeholders

### Development Team
- **Developers**: System development and maintenance
- **Testers**: Quality assurance and testing
- **Technical Lead**: Architecture and technical decisions

### Business Stakeholders
- **Project Owner**: Business requirements and priorities
- **End Users**: Students using the system
- **Administrators**: System administrators (if applicable)

---

## Support Contacts

### Technical Support
- **Development Team**: Technical issues and bugs
- **System Administrators**: System configuration and deployment

### Business Support
- **Project Owner**: Business questions and requirements
- **User Support**: End-user assistance

## Communication Channels

### Development
- **Repository**: Code and documentation
- **Wiki**: System documentation
- **Issue Tracking**: Bug reports and feature requests

### User Support
- **Help Documentation**: User guides and FAQs
- **Support Channels**: User support contact methods

## Escalation Path

3. Project Owner

### Business Issues
1. Project Owner
2. Business Stakeholders
