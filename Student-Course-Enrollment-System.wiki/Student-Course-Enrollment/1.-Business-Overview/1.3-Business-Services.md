## Business Services

The Student Course Enrollment System provides the following core services:

---

## 1. Student Authentication Service

### Purpose
Enable students to securely access the system and manage their enrollments.

### Features
- Student registration
- Secure login
- Session management
- Password security

### Benefits
- Secure access to enrollment system
- Personal account management
- Protected student data

---

## 2. Course Browsing Service

### Purpose
Allow students to discover and explore available courses.

### Features
- Course listing
- Course details
- Search functionality (if implemented)
- Course information display

### Benefits
- Easy course discovery
- Informed enrollment decisions
- Clear course information

---

## 3. Enrollment Management Service

### Purpose
Enable students to enroll in courses and manage their enrollments.

### Features
- Course enrollment
- Enrollment viewing
- Enrollment removal (deregistration)
- Enrollment status tracking

### Benefits
- Self-service enrollment
- Easy enrollment management
- Real-time enrollment status

---

## 4. Course Management Service (If Applicable)

### Purpose
Enable administrators to manage course offerings.

### Features
- Course creation
- Course updates
- Course deletion
- Course information management

### Benefits
- Efficient course management
- Up-to-date course information
- Flexible course administration

---

## Service Integration

All services work together to provide:

- **Seamless Experience**: Integrated services provide smooth user experience
- **Data Consistency**: Shared data ensures consistency across services
- **Security**: Unified authentication protects all services
- **Reliability**: Consistent service availability

---

## Service Quality

All services maintain:

- **Availability**: Services are accessible when needed
- **Performance**: Fast response times
- **Reliability**: Consistent operation
- **Security**: Secure data handling
