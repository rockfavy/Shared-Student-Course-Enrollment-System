# Course Enrollment System – Business Overview
---

# 1. Overview

The Course Enrollment System enables students to register, authenticate, browse courses, enroll in courses, and manage their enrollments through a modern web application.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The system provides:

- **Student Registration & Authentication** – Secure access to the system  
- **Course Browsing** – View all available courses  
- **Course Enrollment** – Enroll in multiple courses  
- **Enrollment Management** – View and manage enrolled courses  
- **Deregistration** – Remove courses from enrollment list  

---

# 3. Key Features

## 3.1 Student Management

- Student registration  
- Student login  
- Student profile management  

## 3.2 Course Management

- View available courses  
- Course details display  
- Course CRUD operations (if needed)  

## 3.3 Enrollment Management

- Enroll in courses  
- View enrolled courses  
- Deregister from courses  

---

# 4. Business Rules

- Students can register and log into the system  
- Students can enroll in multiple courses  
- Courses can have multiple students  
- Students can view their enrolled courses  
- Students can view available courses  
- Students can deregister from courses  

---

# 5. User Roles

## 5.1 Student

- Register and login  
- Browse courses  
- Enroll in courses  
- View enrolled courses  
- Deregister from courses  

---

# 6. Success Criteria

The system is successful when:

- Students can easily register and login  
- Students can browse and find courses  
- Students can enroll in courses without errors  
- Students can view their enrollment status  
- Students can manage their enrollments effectively
